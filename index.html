GOLDEN TEE VISION

At Golden Tee, we are passionate about the art of soap-making. Each bottle of our liquid soap is carefully formulated to provide a rich, lathering, and scented experience. Our products are made with natural, ethically sourced ingredients that are free from harsh or harmful chemicals.

Founded by Mrs Titilope Omotoke, Golden Tee was born out of a desire to offer a better, higher quality soap for everyday use. From the ingredients we select to the packaging we use, every step of our process is designed with care for you.

Thank you for choosing Golden Tee. We’re excited to share our passion for quality and sustainability with you, one bottle at a time.
